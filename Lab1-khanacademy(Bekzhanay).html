Pancakes

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1

Step 1: Mix flour, milk, and egg in a bowl until smooth.

Step 2: Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladle of batter.

Step 3: Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.

Chocolate Mug Cake

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 4 tbsp
Sugar 2 tbsp
Cocoa Powder 2 tbsp

Step 1: Mix flour, sugar, and cocoa powder in a mug.

Step 2: Add milk and stir until smooth.

Step 3: Microwave for 1-2 minutes until cooked.
